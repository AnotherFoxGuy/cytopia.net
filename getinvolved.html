Get Involved With The Cytopia Project

We have a variety of ways for individuals to get involved with the Cytopia project. Those looking to contribute can help refactor code, provide technical support, create new graphics or even help with documentation.

Join the discussion on Discord

Development Team

The development team works on the core functionalities of the game. They are quite literally the bread and butter of the entire project. Without them, there would be no Cytopia!

Learn More

Graphics Team

The graphics team creates all of the graphics you see in Cytopia and on Cytopia.net, just like the core team they are an essential part of the production process

Learn More

Web Development Team

The web development team develops and maintains the website or any other online services that are essential to Cytopia's development life cycle.

Learn More

l10n Team

The localization team works on translating Cytopia to other languages. If you know another language other than English, we welcome you to join the localization team!

Learn More

Audio Engineering Team

The audio engineering team is responsible for creating all of the sound effects and music in Cytopia. If you are handy with a synthesizer then you should come work with us!

Learn More

Marketing Team

The marketing team is responsible for spreading the word about Cytopia. They make use of social media, the website and word of mouth to let people know how awesome Cytopia is!
